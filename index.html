What is Open Data Hub?

The Open Data Hub is a comprehensive collection of open source tools designed to facilitate the entire data science life cycle. The project was developed to take advantage of Kubernetes running on the Red Hat OpenShift® Container Platform. It builds on upstream initiatives such as Data Science Pipelines and Model Mesh Serving to serve as the foundation for Red Hat's internal data science and AI platform. Data scientists can use popular tools such as PyTorch and TensorFlow™,to create and develop models using Jupyter notebooks with ease. This allows teams to spend more time solving critical business needs rather than wasting valuable time installing and maintaining infrastructure.

The Open Data Hub strives to encourage collaboration between data scientists and other stakeholders, while following open source best practices. Furthermore, the open source community is welcome to explore and experiment with the development of intelligent applications
